About Teacher Performance Metrics

These metrics provide insights into teaching effectiveness based on student academic outcomes. Metrics include:

  • Average Score: Mean percentage across all student assessments and exams
  • Pass Rate: Percentage of students achieving passing grades
  • Excellence Rate: Percentage of students scoring 80% or higher
  • Assessments: Number of published assessments out of total created
